# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

**Tool Used:** Canva (canva.com)
**Title of Infographic:** Be Digitally Smart

---

## Topics Covered

1. **What is Digital Literacy?**
   The ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technology safely and responsibly.

2. **Safe Internet Practices**
   - Use strong passwords and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
   - Recognise and avoid phishing links
   - Never share personal information on public platforms

3. **Professional Online Presence**
   Why platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn matter for engineering students, and how to maintain a clean, professional digital identity.

---

## Reflection

I chose Canva because it has a wide range of free templates and icons, making it easy to create something visually appealing quickly. The most challenging part was deciding what to include and what to leave out — digital literacy is a broad topic, and fitting meaningful content into a single page required careful prioritization. I found it genuinely interesting to think about my infographic from the perspective of a student reading it for the first time, which helped me simplify the language and make the layout cleaner.
