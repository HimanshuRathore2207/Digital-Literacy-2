## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic using **Canva** covering: what digital literacy is, safe internet practices, and professional online presence. The infographic is designed for first-year students to quickly understand key digital skills.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**. Each platform serves a different purpose in building an academic and professional digital identity over four years of B.Tech.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner challenge on **HackerRank** and built a 5-question **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using Google Forms — a real tool that can be used to quiz batchmates.

**Google Form (Digital Literacy Quiz):** [https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1xbRAYE4rZXT-efySRUbTv3R6uDZln5qiYlidPfXQikc/edit](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1xbRAYE4rZXT-efySRUbTv3R6uDZln5qiYlidPfXQikc/edit)

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails (to a professor and an internship coordinator) following all email etiquette rules. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist for college students.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched **Cyberbullying** as a cybercrime type, wrote a 200–250 word case study, and created a Stay Safe Online prevention checklist with 8+ tips specific to college students in India.
