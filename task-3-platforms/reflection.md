## Part A – Coding Practice: HackerRank

**Platform:** HackerRank (hackerrank.com)
**Challenge Completed:** Solve Me First (Beginner)

The challenge required reading two integers as input, computing their sum, and printing the result. While the problem itself was simple, it was a valuable introduction to how competitive coding platforms work — understanding how to read problems, write solutions, and submit them for automated evaluation.

| # | Question | Type |
